Southwest Auto Glass

Award winning auto glass service in El Paso, TX

 

History

Southwest Auto Glass, a privately owned company was founded (1992) in El Paso, TX by Rafael Garcia

 

Today

We are the largest and most respected auto glass company in the Southwest. With over 175 years of combined experience behind us, you can be assured of the best possible service and products. Whether you need a windshield for your sports car or door glass for the company truck, we carry every type of glass for every vehicle on the road today.

In 2000 Southwest Auto Glass won the Better Business Bureau's Torch Award for Marketplace Ethics, and was named a finalist in 1998, 1999 and 2001. As the oldest locally owned business specializing in auto glass in El Paso, we recognize that we wouldn't be here if it wasn't for the wonderful support we receive from the community.

On behalf of everyone at Southwest Auto Glass, Thank You El Paso!

 

Mission

To deliver the absolute best value in the communities we serve by offering the highest quality products in the industry at market competitive prices.

Ensuring the success of our company through repeat and referral business achieved by complete customer satisfaction, attention to detail, and the highest regard for the safety of our customers.

 

Why Southwest?

We are the oldest and longest running auto glass company in this region.

  • We have the most experience.  Not only in the trade we've chosen but in the communities we've been blessed to provide it to. 
  • We have built over that time a strong knowledge base, from knowing how our climate affects the various adhesive systems required to what parts to have on hand and ready for when our customers need them.
  • Chances are we've already performed our services for you or somebody you know.  Repeat and referral business is important to us, our goal is to earn your business now and in the future.  There is enough history with us that you can make a well informed choice rather than hope we do a good job.

We are the largest independent auto glass company in the region.

  • We are locally owned and operated.  Our roots run deep within the community.
  • By supporting us you are supporting local business.
  • Being local doesn't mean being smaller,  We compete at every level to provide the best value to our customers.  We have strong alliances with all major insurance companies and OEM auto glass manufacturers. 
  • We are large enough to have the Tools, Training and infrastructure in place to have what our growing community needs and were small enough to always provide one on one great customer service.

We are the only auto glass company registered with the Auto Glass Safety Council in this region.

  • We meet the ANSI/AGSS standard and we are committed to safety and quality at the highest level.
  • We are serious about compliance with the standard of best practices for the safe installation of auto glass.
