Your Local Glass Installation Experts

As the largest and most respected auto glass company in El Paso, Southwest Auto Glass is the company to call for any and all window and windshield repairs and replacements. We're the only auto glass company in El Paso, TX, certified by the Auto Glass Safety Council, so you can rest assured that your vehicle is in good hands.

 

Glass Repair and Replacement Services

Sometimes you just can't avoid damage to your windows. Highway debris, car accidents, and even stray golf balls can cause serious chips and cracks, or even shatter a windshield. When it's time to have yours fixed, you need Southwest Auto Glass. We even offer mobile service and come directly to your home or business! We also work on RV, truck, and fleet vehicles, as well as residential and commercial buildings. We're repaired damage at many car dealerships around El Paso, including Lexus and BMW, and we specialize in foreign vehicles.

 

In Shop or Mobile Auto Glass Replacement

We carry a wide variety of auto glass parts from well-known and respected manufacturers, including PPG, Pilkington, Carlite, Guardian, Mopar, Curtis-Rand, and Burco!

We offer same day glass replacement service, so your car is ready for the road again in no time.
