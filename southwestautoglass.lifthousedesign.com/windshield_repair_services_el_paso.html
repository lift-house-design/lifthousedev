Chip Repair

Why should I repair my chips?

  • It is easier on you. It can usually be done while you wait or while you are at work.
  • It is easier on your vehicle.
  • It is better for the environment.

What is the windshield repair process?

Windshield repair is a permanent process that removes air from the break and replaces it with a durable resin. The process bonds the glass to the resin, restores strength to the windshield, improves the break’s appearance, and prevents the break from spreading.

 

What types of damage can be repaired?

Under 6 (six) inch cracks, bullseyes, chips, star, and combination breaks can be repaired if:

  • The damage fits into an area 3.5” in diameter, or if it fits under a drivers license
  • The damage is not in the critical area (8.5” x 11”area directly in front of the drivers vision)

What do I have to do to have a repair performed?

All you need to do is contact Southwest Auto Glass. Inform the glass specialist you speak to that you have a chip or crack and need a repair made. They will assess the issue and dispatch a glass technician to your location. The cost is only $39.95, and Southwest Auto Glass will help you with your insurance claim. You can also order this service online by clicking order now.

 

Why is it easier on me?

Southwest Auto Glass will come to you with our professional mobile service. This will save you time and frustration in getting out of work to take your vehicle to the shop. Repairs also help reduce insurance costs and therefore could reduce your premiums. In addition, all deductibles are usually waived when you choose to repair your windshield instead of replacing it.

 

Why is it easier on my vehicle?

Glass repair is easier on your vehicle as the factory glass does not have to be removed, thus the factory seal and structural rigidity is not disturbed.

 

Why is it easier on the environment?

Since the glass does not have to be replaced, there is less glass being discarded into landfills.
